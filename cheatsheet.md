## Movement
* tx find character x. put cursor just before x.
* fx find charcter x. put cursor at x.

## Operators
* d: delete
* c: change
* y: copy (yank)
* g~: swap case
* gu: make lowercase
* gU: make uppercase
* >: shift right
* <: shift left
* =: autoindent

## Text Objects
* w: word
* p: paragraph
* ), b, B: paranthesis
* }: curly braces
 
### Actions
* aw: around word
* iw: inner word
* w: till end of word
